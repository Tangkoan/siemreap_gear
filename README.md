# 🎒 Siem Reap Gear - Final Year Project (Sarana) 🎓
**A Specialized Point of Sale (POS) & Inventory Management System for Outdoor Gear.**

Built with **Laravel 11**, this project is my Bachelor's Degree graduation thesis. It is designed to digitize and optimize the operations of outdoor equipment shops in Siem Reap, Cambodia, focusing on sales, rentals, and staff management.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔐 User Management & Security
- **Authentication:** Secure Login and Logout system.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Distinct interfaces and capabilities for Admins, Managers, and Staff.
- **Granular Permissions:** Specific access controls ensuring employees only perform tasks relevant to their responsibilities.

### 🛒 Point of Sale (POS) & Sales
- **Sales Module:** Streamlined checkout process for gear sales and rentals.
- **Transaction History:** Detailed logs of every sale for transparency and auditing.

### 📦 Inventory & Stock Control
- **Stock Management:** Real-time tracking of equipment quantities.
- **Product Categorization:** Easily organize gear by type, brand, or rental/sale status.
- **Stock Alerts:** Stay informed when items are running low.

### 📊 Reporting & Analytics
- **Sales Reports:** Daily, weekly, and monthly insights into business performance.
- **Data Visualization:** Simple and clear summaries of revenue and popular products.
